Orders and Delivery

How long for the food to be delivered/pick up?

Once order is successfully submitted and payment is successfully processed, our restaurant will start preparing the orders. Phone call will be made once our delivery rider is out for deliver or the order is ready for self pick up.

I didn't receive/pick up my order. What should I do?

If you do not receive the order for delivery, please contact us via phone call with contact number provided in the "Contact Us" page. For self pick up order, please reach out to staffs in the restaurant, we will look into this for you immediately.

How can I cancel my delivery/self pick up order?

If you wish to edit order in terms of add on or cut short order, please contact us as soon as possible in order to inform our kitchen regarding this issue. If you wish to cancel order for delivery/self pick up, please contact us. Once confirmed, we will contact you again to inform whether it is successfully cancelled or not. If your orders have been prepared, it is unavailable to be cancelled.

What do I do if my order arrived with missing items?

Please contact us and inform the operator of your missing items in the order. We will arrange for items to be delivered to you in the soonest time possible.

What is the operation hour for delivery service and self pick up service.

Both delivery and self pick up service is 10am - 6pm.
