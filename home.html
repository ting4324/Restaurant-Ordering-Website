Cafe Founded By Programmer

Code. Chill. Coffee

Brew House is a cafe founded by a group of programmers from TARUMT. We strike for providing a chill environment for our customers to enjoy food and coffee. Plugs and power supply are provided, this is the best place for programmers to code.



We make good food.

We believe that delivering the best goes a long way for our beloved customers. From providing the comforts of home within the four walls of our restaurant to provide the best coffee and food, Brew House strives to make every bite worth your while with quality and health in mind.
