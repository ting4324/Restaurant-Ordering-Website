Payments and Security

How can I pay for dine in/delivery/self pick up?

For dine in customers, we do accept cash, Visa and Master credit or debit cards as well as Duitnow E-wallet payment. For delivery and pick up customers, only payments via Visa and Master credit or debit cards will be accepted.

Is my online payment information safe?

The transaction details will not be handled by Brew House Company. In fact, all payment details will be handled by the payment service provider. Your payment details will be send to bank with secure encryption, strict standard and would not be accessed by unauthorized third party (Including Brew House).

Will Brew House store my payment information?

Your payment information will not be handled and accessed by us. All the payment details will be handled by the payment service provider under secure encryption and strict standard.

How and when I will receive the refund for order cancelled?

For cancelled orders, it takes 2-3 business days to process the order and payment information. We will contact bank and refunds will be transfer back via card's account you have submitted during check out.
